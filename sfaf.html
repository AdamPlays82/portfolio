The Project
Redesign the San Francisco AIDS Foundation's website.
The Purpose

The website fails several usability heuristics, consequently making it difficult for users to find important information.

If the SFAF made improvements to the website, they would be more successful at fulfilling their mission to reduce new HIV infections, better the health of people living with HIV, and build stronger communities.

A redesign would not only make San Francisco a healthier place, it could save lives.

The Research

Interviewed two people associated with the SFAF, a co-founder and the director of an HIV testing site.

Researched the history of AIDS and HIV in America, as well as HIV treatment and prevention best practices

Conducted a survey about HIV

Conducted a heuristic analysis of www.sfaf.org

Conducted a competitive analysis of organizations concerned with LGBT lifestyles, HIV, and general health

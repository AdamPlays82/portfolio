The Problem

Transportation in the city of San Francisco is under constant scrutiny from its residents. How might we improve the San Francisco public transportation experience for all its users?

The Team

Adam Simpson

Paula Lam

Justin Kou

The Research
  • Administered two surveys, one for SF residents who use public transportation, and another for those who don't.
  • Conducted 6 user interviews, including a District Supervisor.
  • Questioned 14 users in guerilla interviews while they were in transit.
  • Took note of user behavior at terminals and in transit during ethnographic studies.
Major Insights
  • Users feel that public transit vehicles and stations are unclean.
  • Many users agree that public transit vehicles are overcrowded and too infrequent.
  • Many riders don't enjoy using public transportation in the Bay Area.
Design Strategy
  • How might we create a cleaner public transportation experience for our users?
  • How might we create a public transportation experience that’s roomy and comfortable?
  • How might we create a public transportation experience that’s fun and enjoyable?
Features

Kaboose is a premium service for BART users. Simply purchase an affordable subscription on the Kaboose app to gain access to the Kaboose car. Amenities include:

  • Cleanliness and Safety - Attendants check for tickets and supervise passengers
  • Quiet Time - No-noise policy
  • Roomy Interior - Open bench seating and standing areas
  • Relaxing Ride - Aromatherapy, lemon water, and groovy ceiling display
